Privacy Policy — Delight Plus Studio

Effective date: 2026-02-04

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile apps and games published by Delight Plus Studio on Google Play (the “Apps”).

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at: your-email@example.com

We do not ask users to provide personal information like name, phone number, or address inside our Apps. However, some data may be collected automatically by the app and/or by third-party services (ads/analytics) as described below.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app/game and features used, the following information may be collected automatically:

  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage data (screens viewed, taps, session duration)
  • Diagnostics and crash logs (to fix bugs and improve stability)
  • Identifiers such as Advertising ID (for ads and measurement)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address for ads/analytics)

If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and the information you send in your message.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve our Apps
  • To fix crashes and performance issues
  • To show ads (for free apps that include ads)
  • To measure ad performance and prevent fraud

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our Apps may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you or improve services. These services have their own privacy policies.

Common services we may use:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics (analytics & crash reporting)

You can review Google’s policies here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Advertisements

Some of our Apps show advertisements using third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). These networks may collect information automatically (such as Advertising ID, IP address, device information, and interaction data) to serve and measure ads and to prevent fraud.

We do not directly control how third-party ad networks use your data. Please review the third-party privacy policies for more details.

If you wish, you can limit ad personalization from your device settings (Google settings → Ads / Privacy).

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and the effective date will be updated.
